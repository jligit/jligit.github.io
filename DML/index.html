About

Davis Math Lab connects UC Davis undergraduates with faculty-led research projects in mathematics. Explore project listings created by our faculty, each with a short description and the number of available student spots. Interested students apply by responding to a few qualifying questions and uploading an unofficial transcript.

Applications for Spring 2026 will open in early February 2026 and are due March 1, 2026. Applicants must be current UC Davis undergraduates .
