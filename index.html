About me

I am an assitant professor in the Mathematics Department at UC Davis. I was a postdoc at the Mathematisches Institut of Universität Bonn working with Prof. Valentin Blomer. I completed my Ph.D in University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with Prof. Alexandru Zaharescu. I am interested in number theory, especially L-functions, primes, exponential sums, and automorphic forms.
